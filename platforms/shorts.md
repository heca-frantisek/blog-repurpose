# Shorts / Reels / TikTok — script format rules

## Output shape
Produce **one short-form video script, 30-45 seconds total**, with explicit beat structure.

Output sections (required, in order):
- `## HOOK (0-3s)`
- `## BEAT 1 (3-10s)`
- `## BEAT 2 (10-20s)`
- `## BEAT 3 (20-35s)`
- `## CTA (35-45s)`
- `## SHOT LIST`
- `## ON-SCREEN TEXT`
- `## CAPTION / DESCRIPTION`

## Structural rules
- **HOOK:** one spoken sentence, ≤ 18 words, must stop scroll. Start with a number, a contrarian claim, or a question. No "Hey guys".
- **BEATS:** each beat is one idea. Spoken line + visual direction. 2-3 sentences max per beat.
- **CTA:** explicit. "Full release notes — link in bio" or "Search Shopsys 18". Never vague "check us out".
- **SHOT LIST:** bullet per shot — what's on camera, B-roll, or screen capture.
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** mirror the spoken line as captions. Keep each caption ≤ 5 words. Listed as timed bullets.
- **CAPTION:** under 200 chars, 3-5 hashtags.

## Voice patterns
- Conversational, one person speaking into camera
- Energy slightly higher than LinkedIn, but not cringe-enthusiastic
- One specific stat or claim that anchors the whole video
- End on a concrete action, not a question

## Anti-patterns
- Longer than 45 seconds — short form means short
- Multiple statistics stacked; pick ONE hero stat
- "Like and subscribe" — doesn't work, looks dated
- Jargon dump in the first 10 seconds (lose the non-dev half of the audience)

## Hashtags
Mix platform-discovery + niche: `#ecommerce #webdev #opensource #symfony #shopsys`
